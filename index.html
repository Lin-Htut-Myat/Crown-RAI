What is an Organization?

An organization is a structured group of people working together to achieve common goals. Examples include businesses, non-profits, schools, and government agencies.

Real-Life Example: Apple Inc. is an organization that designs and sells consumer electronics. It operates as an open system, interacting with suppliers, customers, and competitors to innovate and deliver products like the iPhone.
